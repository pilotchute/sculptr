sculptr

Queer scientists, particularly trans scientists, need community more than ever. Sculptr is a project started by Alea Kootz to address this unmet need. The goal of sculptr is to build community and mutual support through concious and deliberate effort. This project will help queer scientists be more resilient in the face of discrimination.

  • contact sculptr

Alea Kootz (she/her)

The founder of sculptr, who felt a desperate need for socialization, community, and mutual support among queer scientists. She was working to address that need before she was discriminated and harassed out of her dream job. She used her time between positions to create a more organized and intentional structure for the growing community she started. This website, and the additional materials it supports, are a part of that effort.

Support sculptr

Sculptr socially supports approximately 50 people at events each month. We work hard to keep the cost per person very low, but the cost for COVID tests and food for events does add up. Any donations are invested into the community, based on the needs that are most pressing. We are currently able to accept venmo and paypal, we hope to be able to accept other options soon. <3
